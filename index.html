Aljosa Osep, Ph.D.

Hi, I'm Aljosa, and I come from the Alpine side of Slovenia. I obtained my M.Sc. degree at the University of Bonn and Ph.D. at RWTH Aachen University under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Bastian Leibe. Currently, I am working with Prof. Dr. Laura Leal-Taixe (Dynamic Vision and Learning Group) at the Technical University of Munich.
